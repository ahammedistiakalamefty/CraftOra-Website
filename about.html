About Us

Welcome to CraftOra! Founded in 2026, our passion for handmade creations began with a simple idea—to bring unique, sustainable art into everyday life.

CraftOra is a handmade product shop where we create and sell unique rings, earrings, necklaces, and artistic glass jars.

Meet our team: A group of five passionate students from Daffodil International University — Ahammed Istiak Alam Efty, Jannatul Mawa Jui, Mariya Akter, Aman Ullah Asif, and Tanjina Mahi.

Thank you for supporting small businesses and handmade art!
